## Logging In

1. Go to your website URL
2. Click **"Login"**
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click **"Sign In"**

### Upgrade/Renew
1. Click **"Subscribe"** or **"Upgrade"**
2. Enter payment info
3. Subscription activates automatically

## When Something Goes Wrong

### Can't Log In?
1. Check email/password (no typos)
2. Click "Forgot Password?" to reset
3. Still stuck? Contact developer

### Website Won't Load?
1. Refresh page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
2. Try different browser
3. Check internet connection
4. Still down? Contact developer

### Emails Not Coming?
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Wait 5-10 minutes (sometimes delayed)
3. Verify email address is correct
4. Still not working? Contact developer

### Subscription Issues?
1. Check subscription status in Settings
2. Wait 5-10 minutes (updates can be delayed)
3. Verify payment went through (check bank/Stripe)
4. Still not working? Contact developer immediately
